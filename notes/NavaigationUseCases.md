# Navigation Use-Cases

## Origins & Targets
- different locations
    - relative to surface
    - relative to orbit
    - relative to system
    - bookmarked position
- different time

## Navigation Scenarios
- same planet
    - surface-surface
    - surface-orbit / orbit-surface
    - orbit-orbit
- different planet
    - surface-surface
    - surface-orbit
    - orbit-surface
    - orbit-orbit
- free positions
    - surface-position
    - position-surface
    - orbit-position
    - position-orbit
    - position-position
- different point in time
    - timepoint-timepoint (same position forward/backward)
    - combined w/ above navigation scenarios
